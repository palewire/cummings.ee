Portraits II

of evident invisibles
exquisite the hovering

at the dark portals

of hurt girl eyes

 

sincere with wonder

a poise a wounding
a beautiful supression

the accurate boy mouth

 

now droops the faun head

now the intimate flower dreams

of parted lips
dim upon the syrinx
