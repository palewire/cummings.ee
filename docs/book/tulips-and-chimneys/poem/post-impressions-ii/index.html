Post Impressions II

the moon is hiding in
her hair,
The lily
of heaven
full of all dreams,
drawns down.

cover her briefness in singing
close her with intricate faint birds
by daisies and twilights
Deepen her,

Recite
upon her
flesh
the rain’s

pearls singly-whispering

The poem was first published in “Tulips and Chimneys.”
